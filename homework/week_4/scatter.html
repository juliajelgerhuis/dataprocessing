Life Expectancy vs. Gross National Income

Julia Jelgerhuis - 10725482

This scatterplot shows the relationship between the life expectancy in years at birth and the gross national income per country. Furthermore, these two variables are also compared against average hours worked per year, which you can see by looking at the size of dots. Source: stats.oecd.org. You can choose the year you want to see in the dropdown menu under this text. 0 = 2011, 1 = 2012, 2 = 2013, 3 = 2014 and 4 = 2015.
